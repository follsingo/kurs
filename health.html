Covid 19 Insurance

Benefits of insurance in Quiet Harbor:

  • High level of insurance protection with small insurance premiums
  • In case of coronavirus infection, the insurance payment, depending on the duration and type of treatment, can amount to up to 20% of the insured amount
  • Discounts depending on the number of insured, regular customers, for break-even insurance, if there are contracts for other types of voluntary insurance
  • The possibility of paying the insurance premium in installments

Insurance of credit recipients

A loan can easily improve life "here and now". You can buy a car, make repairs, go on vacation... But at the same time there is a risk of being in a financial trap: an accident, health problems can deprive you of the opportunity to work and repay the loan. To protect yourself and your loved ones from financial problems, an insurance policy for voluntary insurance against accidents and illnesses of credit recipients will help – an inexpensive and easiest way to repay a loan in a critical situation.

Accident and disease insurance

An insurance policy for voluntary insurance against accidents and diseases is a simple and inexpensive way to provide yourself and your loved ones with guaranteed financial protection in the event of injury or surgical intervention (except outpatient surgery) as a result of a newly diagnosed disease.

Conclude a compulsory health insurance contract

The insurance company protects the rights of the insured to receive free medical care in the amount and conditions specified in the territorial program of compulsory medical insurance. Within the framework of the State Guarantees Program, they are provided free of charge:

  • Primary health care
  • Emergency medical care
  • Specialized medical care
  • High-tech medical care
  • Palliative care
  • Medical rehabilitation

Benefits of voluntary health insurance

  • The ability to make an appointment with specialized specialists without long queues and many unsuccessful attempts to get a ticket at the polyclinic
  • Protection against price changes for various medical services
  • Guarantee of receiving a full range of treatment in accordance with your chosen health insurance program
